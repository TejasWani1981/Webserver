What is Kubernetes?

Kubernetes is an open-source platform for automating the deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications. It helps manage containerized workloads and services, enabling easy scaling and operational efficiency.

Key Features of Kubernetes:

  • Automated deployment and scaling of containers.
  • Self-healing, with automatic replacement of failed containers.
  • Load balancing and service discovery for containerized apps.
